1. Data Controller

The data controller for personal data is Niccolò Angioloni, based in Italy. For questions: keymatichelpdesk@gmail.com

2. Types of Data Collected

  • Registration Data: email and password for account creation; login email and password cannot be changed

  • Stored Credentials: usernames and passwords saved by user, encrypted and inaccessible to owner

Users are responsible for any personal data of third parties shared through the App.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Provide KeyMatic service and manage user accounts
  • Securely store credentials in a protected cloud
  • Handle communications and provide technical support
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Anonymous analysis of service performance and security
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

Data is not used for profiling, advertising, or third-party sales.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract execution (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)
  • Explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for encrypted credential storage

5. Processing Methods

  • Encryption of stored passwords
  • Multi-factor authentication (verified email)
  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • Periodic backups in protected areas
  • Access limited to authorized personnel

6. Data Retention

  • Registration Data: until account deletion
  • Encrypted Credentials: deleted on account removal
  • Technical and security logs: retained max 12 months unless required by law

Account deletion permanently and irreversibly removes all data from active and backup systems.

7. Data Sharing and Transfer

Data is not shared with third parties except:

  • Use of Firebase (Google LLC) as external data processor

Transfers outside the EU comply with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) of the European Commission.
